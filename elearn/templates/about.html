Want to know about us ?

This is SUST LMS, Django web based online learning management system developed by Md. Shahriar Parvez and S.M. Mahmudul Hasan student of SUST. The main aim is to provide SUST LMS to maintain a Learning Management System for all of the students from different departments across the university. For that purpose, people can register in different courses of their interests and learn from their teachers.
